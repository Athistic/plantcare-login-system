Introduction

We love planting trees and crops,but we sometimes forget to take care of them due to unforseen circumstances or being occupied by our profession of work. This inevitably results in plants dying and some not producing what is expected by the farmer since they lack certain nutrients and care.

Thus we bring you a game changing application that can keep you up to date with the progress of a variety of crops you may wish to plant and also helps you to interact with other farmers like you.
